Privacy Policy

🔒 Last updated: June 12, 2026 | Version 2.0 — "Radial Transparency"

At NeoGrid Marketplace, your privacy is at the core of our orbit. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Introduction & Scope

NeoGrid Marketplace ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy describes our practices regarding the collection, storage, usage, disclosure, and security of information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our services (collectively, the "Platform"). This policy applies to all users, including visitors, registered customers, and contributors.

🌿 The "Aether Pledge": We only collect data that helps us improve your experience, and we never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless shopping experience, we may collect the following categories of personal data:

📝 Identity Data
Name, username, date of birth (optional), and profile preferences.
📬 Contact Data
Email address, shipping/billing addresses, phone number.
💳 Transaction Data
Purchase history, payment method details (tokenized via secure PCI providers).
🌐 Technical Data
IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies, and browsing behavior.

Special categories (sensitive data)

We do not knowingly collect sensitive information such as health data, political opinions, or biometric data. If you voluntarily provide such details (e.g., in product reviews), you consent to its limited processing as described.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order fulfillment: Processing payments, shipping products, and providing order updates.
  • Account management: Creating and maintaining your NeoGrid profile, wishlists, and reward programs.
  • Customer support: Responding to inquiries, returns, and technical issues.
  • Marketing & personalization (opt-in): Sending tailored offers, new collection alerts, and site recommendations. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Security & fraud prevention: Monitoring suspicious activities and ensuring platform integrity.
  • Legal compliance: Fulfilling tax, regulatory, or law enforcement requests.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & CCPA Alignment)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill purchases and provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving our platform, fraud detection, and direct marketing (with opt-out).
  • Consent: For optional communications and cookie tracking where required.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws and tax regulations.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information. NeoGrid does not sell your data; however, you may exercise your CCPA rights via the contact section below.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies ("NebulaTrace")

We use cookies, web beacons, and local storage to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through our consent manager or your browser settings. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they enable core features (e.g., cart, checkout). For detailed information, refer to our Cookie Preferences tool available on the site.

🍪 Third-party cookies: We use analytics partners (e.g., privacy-friendly analytics) and payment processors who set their own cookies. These are governed by their respective policies.

6. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Service providers: Shipping carriers, payment gateways (Stripe/PayPal), cloud hosting, and email platforms — under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy, your data may be transferred as an asset.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of NeoGrid, our users, or the public.

7. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard safeguards including SSL encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. While no system is 100% secure, we strive to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, typically:

  • Account data: Retained until you request deletion, plus a limited period for legal obligations (e.g., tax records up to 7 years).
  • Transaction history: 7 years for financial compliance.
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt out or unsubscribe.

8. Your Privacy Rights ("Orbit Control")

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure (Right to be forgotten): Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention limits.
  • Restriction & Portability: Limit processing or receive a machine-readable copy of your data.
  • Opt-out of marketing: Unsubscribe from promotional emails via the "unsubscribe" link or account settings.
  • Withdraw consent: For processing based solely on consent (e.g., cookies).

To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@neogrid.market. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU/EEA, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children's Privacy

NeoGrid Marketplace is not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us to request removal.

10. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access our platform from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US. We ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place for data transfers from the EEA/UK. By using NeoGrid, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in legal requirements, our data practices, or platform features. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. Material changes will be notified via email or prominent website banner. Your continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
