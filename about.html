More than a marketplace.
A movement for mindful living.

NeoGrid was born from a simple belief: the objects we surround ourselves with should be as kind to the planet as they are beautiful. We curate independent designers, ethical manufacturers, and timeless pieces that bridge sustainability and modern aesthetics.

What started as a small collective of designers and environmental advocates has grown into a global platform — but our core remains the same: transparency, craftsmanship, and respect for the earth's resources.
